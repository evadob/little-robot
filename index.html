About Robik

Once upon a time, there was a little robot named Robik. One day, he set off on an adventure to the human city. On his journey, he encountered a malevolent robot named Trpaslíktron, who wanted to take control of the city. Robik was clever, a very clever one indeed. He discovered that Trpaslíktron was dependent on old batteries, which hindered his enthusiasm for new things. So, Robik gathered all the old batteries in the city and devised a plan.

Výhra

As Trpaslíktron sought power, Robik unleashed a massive fireworks, completely overwhelming Trpaslíktron. In that moment, Robik swiftly replaced his old batteries with new ones, and Trpaslíktron transformed into a friendly robot. The people were thrilled and praised Robik's abilities. He became their hero, and together with Trpaslíktron, they worked together to make the city a better place.

Alfi

One day, a circus arrived in the city, featuring special robotic performances. The star attraction was a magical robot named Alfi. She was a stunning robotic lady who could perform mesmerizing tricks with laser beams and had a beautiful robotic voice for singing. When Robik saw Alfi for the first time, he was captivated by her elegance and artistry. He felt a desire to get to know her, but he was too shy and unsure how to approach her. Nevertheless, he decided to observe her from afar and attempted to find a way to enter her world.
